Sources

Below is a list of sources that helped with the creation of this website.

Books

The book Anime: A History helped immensely when writing the evolution and history part of this website as well as several others. This book was written by Jonathan Clements in 2013.

The Anime Encyclopedia 3rd revised edition by Jonathan Clements and Helen McCarthy was a big help throughout the site as well. It was published in 2015.

Anime Fan Communities: Transcultural Flows and Frictions by Sandra Annett was also used to create this website. It was published in 2014

Other Websites

MyAnimeList has a wealth of information and was often my first stop for looking up stuff about specific anime and community opinions.

This websited by Owlcation was also very helpful for the glossary section.

Images and Other Media

Many of the images used on this site came from the TVDB database that has a vast collection of anime cover art and fan art. All credit for the images goes to those that created the originals.

Several Youtube videos were linked to throughout the site. Disclaimer: I do not own any of the content in the videos linked, nor did I create any of them personally. Again, all credit goes to the creators.
