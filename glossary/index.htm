Glossary of Anime Terms and Phrases

Are you confused with the anime community's vocabulary? Have you come across words or phases and have no clue what they could possibly mean? Well, welcome to the world of anime.

Do you feel like this?

Suffice to say, every fandom has its own strange vocabulary that can initially freak out and confuse newcomers. However, not every fandom insists on using terms and phrases from multiple languages; but unfortunately (or fortunately depending on how you look at it) the process of becoming a hardcore anime fan often involves learning a bit of Japanese.

So, don't worry, it's okay to be confused with the anime vernacular; to be entirely honest many dedicated fans are probably in the same boat (hey there, how's it going?).

Hopefully this glossary will help you out some, but if you're really stuck then maybe try using a translator; it's better than nothing. Though, in the end, learning these terms and phrases is not necessary for the enjoyment of anime, you'll be perfectly fine without knowing any of them! However, if you truly wish to understand the anime community and immerse yourself in the Japanese culture then perhaps knowing some of these terms and phrases will be just what you need.
