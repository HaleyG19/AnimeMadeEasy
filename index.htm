Hey there! What brings you here today? Are you:

New to anime?

Well, if you find yourself answering yes to any of the above questions then you've come to the right place! So, take a seat, make yourself comfortable, and let's get started!

Here's some information about each section, to help you get orientated:

Evolution and History:

This section provides an overview of the evolution and history of anime from its inception in 1907 to what it has become today. If you are a history buff, or just want to know how the medium has changed throughout the years then this section is definitely for you.

Mainstream Misconceptions:

This section discusses several common misconceptions about anime that deter many people from delving into the medium. Rule #1: Anime is not just for kids!

Glossary:

This section provides a beginner's guide to terms and phrases frequently used in anime and by the anime community. Hopefully this will help newcomers to anime feel less overwhelmed by the lingo so they can overcome those nervous feelings that come with delving into anything new.

Where do I Start?:

This section breaks down the medium and explores common genres, popular shows, my personal favourites, and provides helpful links and resources to further address the needs of anime fans and newcomers.
